How It Works

The ELO Rating System

The Line uses the ELO rating system — the same model used in chess and competitive esports — to rank players based on match outcomes. Every win and loss moves your rating. Beat a higher-ranked player and you gain more. Lose to a lower-ranked player and you drop more. Rankings reflect true skill, not just wins.

📈

ELO Gain / Loss Formula

Each player starts at 1200. After a match, points transfer based on expected outcome. Upset wins reward more ELO; expected wins reward less. The K-factor is set to 32, meaning max swing per match is ±32 points.

🎯

Match Format

All ranked matches are best-of-3 games to 11 points. Win by 2 is enforced. A match only counts toward ELO if both players confirm the result. Unconfirmed matches are voided.

🔄

Live Updates

Rankings update in real time after each confirmed match. Movement arrows show rank change from the previous session. The 🔥 Hot badge is awarded for gaining 15+ ELO in a single day.

⚠️

Inactivity Penalty

Players who go 14+ days without a ranked match lose 5 ELO per day until their next match. This keeps the leaderboard active and reflects current form, not past glory.
